About Us

The Victoria Drive Society Safety Alliance is a non-profit organization that acts as a bridge between the community, public agencies, and law enforcement to strengthen safety through building relationships and promoting active community engagement along Victoria Drive.

Our Motto

Stronger Community, Safer Spaces

Mission Statement

  • S — Support: Support community-based safety initiatives through observation, outreach, and active engagement to promote unity and participation.
  • A — Advocate: Advocate for collaboration among residents, businesses, public agencies, and law enforcement to build trust and strengthen local safety efforts.
  • F — Foster: Foster strong, inclusive relationships within the community to encourage shared responsibility and collective well-being.
  • E — Educate: Educate and empower stakeholders through safety programs and initiatives that create a more connected and resilient neighbourhood.
